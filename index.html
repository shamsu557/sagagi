Who We Are

A Trusted Agro Enterprise in Northern Nigeria

HB Sagagi Agro and General Enterprises Limited is a Kano-based agricultural enterprise dedicated to production, processing, and distribution of quality agro-products and livestock services. We operate with integrity, professionalism, and innovation.

Registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020, Federal Republic of Nigeria, we are committed to advancing sustainable agriculture and empowering our communities.

🎯 Our Vision

To become a leading agro-based enterprise in Nigeria known for quality and sustainability.

🌾 Our Mission

To provide safe, healthy, and affordable agricultural products while empowering communities.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive agricultural services delivering value from farm to table across Kano State and beyond.

Fish Farming (Aquaculture)

Modern fish pond management and sustainable aquaculture practices producing high-quality catfish and tilapia for local markets.

Livestock Production

Professional rearing of cattle, goats, and poultry using best practices to ensure healthy, market-ready animals.

Agro Processing

Processing of grains, legumes, and agricultural commodities into value-added products for domestic consumption and trade.

Agricultural Product Supply

Reliable supply chain and distribution of agro-products including grains, dried fish, and indigenous food items.

Agricultural Partnerships

Strategic collaboration with farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses to expand agricultural productivity and impact.

General Agro Enterprises

Diversified agro-enterprise activities including procurement, consultancy, and market linkages across the value chain.

Our Products

Quality Agro Products

Authentic, nutritious, and sustainably sourced agricultural products from the heart of Kano State.

Ridi

Traditional Grain Food

A staple grain-based food made from millet or sorghum. Rich in nutrients and deeply rooted in Northern Nigerian culinary tradition.

Zomo

Wild Hare

Premium wild hare sourced sustainably. A lean, protein-rich delicacy prized in traditional Hausa cuisine for its unique flavour.

Bushashen Kifi

Dried Fish

Sun-dried and smoked fish prepared with traditional methods. A vital source of protein and a key ingredient in Northern Nigerian soups and stews.

Kiwon Kifi

Fish Farming / Aquaculture

Fresh farm-raised fish from our managed ponds. We produce catfish and tilapia using sustainable aquaculture techniques.

Dabbobi

Livestock

Healthy cattle and goats raised under professional husbandry standards. Available for market supply, festive seasons, and wholesale trade.

Gyada

Groundnut

High-quality groundnuts from Kano farmlands. Used for oil production, groundnut cake, and as a popular everyday snack.

Daddawa

African Locust Bean Seasoning

Fermented African locust bean (Parkia biglobosa) — an aromatic, protein-rich seasoning at the heart of traditional West African cooking.
